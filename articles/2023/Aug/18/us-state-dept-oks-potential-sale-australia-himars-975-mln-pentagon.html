US State Dept OKs potential sale to Australia of HIMARS for $975 mln - Pentagon

Aug 18, 2023
A M142 High Mobility Artillery Rocket System (HIMARS) takes part in a military exercise near Liepaja, Latvia September 26, 2022. REUTERS/Ints Kalnins/File Photo

The U.S. State Department has approved a possible sale to Australia of M142 High Mobility Artillery Rocket Systems (HIMARS) for $975 million, the Pentagon said on Friday.

The principal contractors will be Lockheed Martin (LMT.N), L3Harris (LHX.N), Leonardo DRS, and Oshkosh (OSK.N), the Pentagon said in a statement.
