Putin and Iran's Raisi hold call, discuss possible Iranian membership of BRICS -TASS

Aug 17, 2023
Russian President Vladimir Putin chairs a meeting with members of the government via video link at the Kremlin in Moscow, Russia August 16, 2023. Sputnik/Mikhail Klimentyev/Kremlin via REUTERS

Russian President Vladimir Putin and his Iranian counterpart Ebrahim Raisi discussed Iran's possible future membership of the BRICS grouping during a phone call on Thursday, Russian state news agency TASS reported, citing the Kremlin.

The BRICS grouping of emerging economies - comprising Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa - is due to discuss its possible expansion at a summit in South Africa next month. Putin and Raisi also reaffirmed their support for further developing bilateral ties in trade, transport and logistics, and energy, TASS reported.
